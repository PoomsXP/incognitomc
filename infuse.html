Infuse Tutorial

There are two types of Infuses, Normal and Augumented. Augumented infuses can be crafted once and normal infuses twice. Augumented effects are more powerful versions of the normal ones that can be obtained by being the first person to craft them and waiting for a 15 minute ritual. After someone obtains a Augumented infuse, that infuse will switch to normal infuses from then on, even if that infuse is destroyed.

How to craft Infuses

  • Place a brewing stand, this is important as infuses can only be crafted inside it
  • Open the brewing stand and select the crafting table
  • Pull up the recipe for the Infuse you want to craft (They are in this channel)
  • Arrange the materials exactly in the crafting grid
  • If an item doesn't show up, the recipe is wrong or the infuse has already been crafted the max amount of times
  • If you are crafting an augumented effect, a 15 minute ritual will start. DO NOT LEAVE THE SERVER OR AREA DURING THE CRAFTING RITUAL! You will lose your infuse and it will cause server issues.
