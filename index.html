Infuse SMP

In Infuse SMP, players craft abilities called Infuses. Each Infuse is limited. There are Normal and Augmented Infuses. Augmented Infuses are stronger and can be crafted once only by the first player, requiring a 15-minute ritual. After that, the Infuse becomes Normal-only, craftable twice total. Infuses are crafted inside a brewing stand with exact recipes. Leaving during an Augmented ritual fails the Infuse and breaks progression. Infuse SMP rewards speed, precision, and control.

Fracture SMP

In Fracture SMP, every player spawns with one random, unstable power. Abilities are strong but unpredictable — using them too much causes glitches and chaos. Powers can rewind time, break gravity, summon clones, phase through walls, or warp resources, often with dangerous side effects. The world itself can fracture: server-wide anomalies, evolving or degrading powers, and rare Artifacts of Stability that grant short-term control. Fracture SMP replaces Lifesteal with high-risk, high-reward chaos where nothing is fully under your control.
