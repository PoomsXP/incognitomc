Rules

These rules are in place to maintain order and make sure everyone feels safe, respected and is having fun!

Minecraft Specific Rules

  • No lag machines
  • No bugs or exploits
  • No hacking/cheating
  • No spawn killing players
  • No xraying
  • No using replay mods for anything besides recording yourself
  • No Naked Killing players (no armor/weapons, not fighting back)

Server-Wide Rules (Discord & Minecraft)

  • No excessive swearing
  • No discrimination of any kind
  • No spamming
  • No threats
  • Keep it PG-13

The Owner or Manager has the final say in all punishments. Not all rules are listed here, nor do staff need a specific rule violation to issue punishment. Moderator/Admin punishments can be appealed via ticket.

By following these rules, everyone can have a fun, respectful and safe experience!
